About Me

While preparing for GATE, I discovered my true passion: technology and coding. I found myself drawn to computer science and its wonders. After graduation, I decided to switch paths and become a software developer. I started preparing for the GATE CS paper with only basic C programming knowledge. In just seven months, I managed to secure an AIR of 2416 out of 123,967 students in GATE 2024 CS and an AIR of 2571 out of 39,210 students in GATE 2024 DA.

I'm confident in my skills, strategies, and hard work. Currently, I'm learning web development and have already created this website using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. I believe that with consistent effort, I can develop professional, industry-level websites. I also have an interest in cybersecurity, but for now, I'm focusing on web development.
